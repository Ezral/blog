Doctor density per 1,000 population

By Ezral on Jun 24, 2020

Jakarta is one cities with the highest doctor density (per 1,000 population) which is comparable to Singapore or Seoul that have around 2.3 to 2.4 doctors per 1,000 population.

Available statistics show that over 45% of WHO Member States report to have less than 1 physician per 1,000 population. — World Health Organization
